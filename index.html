Rules

You don't know how to play? Don't worry! Read the following instructions and have fun!
  • Roll the dice and add the number to your current score. You can either:
    • Keep rolling, adding new points to your current score
    • Hold, adding your points to your total score
  • If you roll a one (1), you lose all your current points
  • Who first reaches 100 points, wins the game
